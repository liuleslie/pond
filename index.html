Transcript

good evening, tonight I want to talk about life-sustaining things

it’s been a tumultuous few months since May. leaving school has been a relief... though I’m not sure whether I move on too hastily

all this time before* me
*or maybe it’s in a different direction for you

lately a question swimming around in my head has been, what is it that I want so badly in / from this life?

I browse the web and come across so many nakedly honest diary entries and am continually in awe of the urge and the need to express

there has been chatter from folks online about the sheer embarrassment that comes with being human
Are there moments where you regard yourself as a friend?

I have thought over what I do and make, and by extension how I am, as a sort of practise

there are many parts to it (it seems it takes quite a while for me to process things in full), but it isn’t quite clear to me whether there is a bigger picture to be found

Someone suggested writing it out

I think I feel most comfortable in a state of quietness, when I can be sentimental and a little aspirational with the feelings I want to understand
/ much has been discussed about the value of conversation...

hm, what might I say I am interested in at the moment, and why?

  • connection... and solitude. anticipating and thinking about final moments of a life — types of love, platonic love especially
  • enchantment and desire, and how attraction works, to a lesser extent. — something about community...

For a while during louder, more grating moments I would want to think about aesthetics and uncanny online phenomena... I would also otherwise want to learn more about the way information looks, the aesthetics of diagramming — power — but I think these themes will be dormant for now, as I find a base state I can reliably return to, instead of these extreme mood swings (haha)
